Innovation in Finance?

He forgot about ETFs and index funds. :) At Stockflare, we want to change the way the entire planet saves and invests. A positive change, where we all save more, smarter, invest easily, sensibly and in the long run, improve the lives of everyone on the planet. Financially, and maybe even emotionally.
