Innovation in Finance?

Paul Volker, former head of the Federal Reserve joked at the height of the financial crisis that the ATM is the only positive innovation in finance in the last 25 years. It's a pertinent half-truth. He forgot about ETFs and index funds. :) At Stockflare, we want to change the way the entire planet saves and invests. A positive change, where we all save more, smarter, invest easily, sensibly and in the long run, improve the lives of everyone on the planet. Financially, and maybe even emotionally.
